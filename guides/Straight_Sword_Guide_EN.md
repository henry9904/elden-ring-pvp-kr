## 📊 Overview

Straight swords are among the **best weapons in PvP**, solidly **S-tier** when used in a powerstance (PS SS). Most of their moveset is fast but still barely reactable. When paired, they can deal tremendous damage in a very short time, offering some of the **highest DPS in the game**.

## 🔍 Weapon Discussion

### 🥇 Coded Sword *(S-Tier)*

Deals **pure holy damage** — pure damage beats split damage, and affinity damage beats physical. The Coded Sword is the **second-longest** and effectively the **highest effective damage** straight sword, at very low weight.

Built for a pure FTH setup, unlocking **Black Flame's Protection** and other powerful buffs. If an opponent casts Divine Fortification, simply swap to a flame-infused sword and cast Black Flame's Protection — you will always have higher damage regardless of what the opponent buffs with.

Its weapon art is too slow for competitive use, making it ideal as an **offhand weapon**. PS SS often doesn't need a weapon art anyway.

> [!NOTE]
> Holyproof Dried Liver would be an excellent counter, but it is **banned in ladder** due to a bug that gives it 35% holy negation instead of the intended 15%.

---

### 🥈 Noble's Slender Sword *(A-Tier)*

The **longest straight sword**, infusible, and very high damage on DEX builds. Overall, the best straight sword without unusual properties.

---

### Carian Knight's Sword *(A-Tier)*

Slightly shorter than Noble's Slender with roughly equal effective damage. Features a unique **Block-Hit R2** — functions as a quasi-shield-poke, blocking opponent moves and striking back in one motion. **The best R2 of any straight sword.** Unfortunately paired with Carian Grandeur, largely useless in PvP.

---

### Golden Epitaph *(A-Tier)*

Outstanding range and damage — slightly behind other A-tier swords in both, but still excellent. Its buffing weapon art adds **+25 holy damage and +5% all damage** for 60 seconds, making it one of the highest-damage options when buffed.

---

### Miquellan Knight's Sword *(B-Tier)*

One of the longest straight swords but comparably low base damage. With **Sacred Blade** buff (+90 holy damage), it compensates well. The buff was improved in 1.07 to last **40 seconds** (up from 20). Also has a unique R2 with considerable travel distance when charged.

---

### Warhawk's Talon *(B-Tier)*

Identical damage to Noble's Slender on optimal stats, but **significantly shorter** and 0.5 weight lighter. Only reason to choose it over Noble's Slender: unique R2 that strikes twice with roll-catching potential.

---

### Rotten Crystal Sword *(B-Tier)*

High-damage and decently long. Key feature: **innate 50 Scarlet Rot buildup** (100 in powerstance). Especially threatening at higher latency due to phantom hit buildup. Has Spinning Slash — excellent for straight swords and great for status application.

---

### Crystal Sword *(B-Tier)*

Very similar to Rotten Crystal Sword but trades the 50 rot buildup for slightly higher AR — generally a bad trade. Highest AR straight sword after Sword of N&F with much better effective damage.

---

### Broadsword *(B-Tier)*

Highest-damage infusible straight sword. Scales with any affinity, useful across builds. However, it's short, and CR1 / R2 are not thrusts (no pierce damage). Best used as an offhand weapon if at all.

---

### Sword of Night and Flame *(C-Tier)*

Awkward INT/FTH build and deceptively high AR, but very low effective damage due to **split across three damage types**. Essentially a worse Rotten Crystal Sword with no status buildup.

---

### Sword of St. Trina *(C-Tier)*

Short, poor damage. Key feature: **65 innate sleep buildup** (130 paired) — can proc sleep in 3–4 hits depending on Clarifying Horn Charm. Even phantom hits build sleep, making this devastating at high latency. On proc, punish with L1 L1 for massive damage and reload buildup. Can hard-swap to Stormhawk Axe for burst damage.

> [!CAUTION]
> Against a knowledgeable opponent using Clarifying Charm and boluses, sleep may never proc — leaving you with very short, very low damage straight swords.

---

### Ornamental Straight Sword *(D-Tier)*

Lowest physical damage, short range. However: **buffing weapon art (+85 holy damage)** and unique **paired moveset when two-handed** (similar to PS SS but with thrusting R2 and a PS thrusting sword JR1). Only 3 weight total — lighter than PS Codeds at 5.

> [!NOTE]
> This weapon is a loophole — it does **not** count as PS SS, allowing you to **ignore the 65 poise limit** in ladder matches.

### Powerstanced (PS) ⭐

| Move | PvP Poise Dmg | Attack MV |
|------|:-------------:|:---------:|
| L1 | 40.5 + 40.5 | 92 + 92 |
| RL1 | 40.5 + 40.5 | 100 + 95 |
| CL1 | 40.5 + 40.5 | 85 + 85 |
| JL1 | 101.25 + 101.25 | 105 + 105 |
| BL1 | 40.5 + 40.5 | 85 + 85 |

The PS moveset is **insanely powerful** due to two moves in particular:

#### 🔑 L1

Strikes with both swords simultaneously. **L1 1** is the best move — massive travel distance that reaches far further than straight swords suggest. L1 3 is the only move that strikes separately (two thrusts) and is generally considered the weakest. **Use L1 1 and L1 2 in most engagements.**

#### 🔑 JL1 *(God-Tier)*

> Greater MV than L1 · Guaranteed poise break in ladder matches · Extremely fast · Favorable X-shaped hitbox · Proceeds into L1 2 · Very low recovery

The most powerful move in the moveset. Only downside: pressing L1 too early may overshoot crouching opponents; height differences and neutral jumps can weaken it.

#### CL1 *(Weak — Avoid)*

> [!CAUTION]
> CL1 lacks L1 1's travel distance and has lower motion values — a **major vulnerability** after rolling. Alternatives:
> - Use **CR1** for greater range
> - Wait to use L1 1 again
> - Jump out of a roll → JL1
> - Backstep out of a roll → BL1

#### BL1

Low motion values, but compensated by travel distance and pierce damage vs. CL1. A circular quickstep around the opponent followed by BL1 roll-catches many panic rolls.

---

## 📋 Summary

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **All movesets suffer from low poise damage**, but the PS moveset compensates with overwhelming damage output.
> **L1 1** and **JL1** are the two moves that win most rounds. You can carry many fights using only these.
