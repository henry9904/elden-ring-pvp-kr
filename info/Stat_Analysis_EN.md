## 🧬 Build Type Overview

| Build | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| 🔨 **Strength** | Wide weapon selection. 1.5x Strength bonus when two-handing. Good Heavy infusion efficiency | Attack patterns can become predictable |
| ⚡ **Dexterity** | High utility with unique weapons | Narrower weapon selection and lower stat efficiency compared to Strength |
| 🔮 **Intelligence** | Can use both melee and sorceries | Must sacrifice Endurance or other stats. Less advantage than Faith when using melee |
| ✨ **Faith** | Best flat damage output, strong damage buff options | Must sacrifice Endurance or other stats |
| 🩸 **Arcane** | Efficient for status effects (Bleed, Poison, Sleep) and Physical+Elemental weapons | Low damage on non-Arcane scaling weapons |
| 🧬 **Hybrid (STR/DEX + Attribute)** | High STR/DEX base, wide weapon variety, maximises damage on specialised weapons | Damage can be low outside specific weapon picks |

## 📊 Stat Breakpoint Analysis

> [!NOTE]
> **Methodology** — Data collected on a Wretch starting class, levelling one stat to 99 while tracking changes. All equipment removed to eliminate variables.
> Weapon upgrade level (e.g. +25) does not change breakpoint locations — it only increases scaling multipliers.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Stats are most efficiently invested at the **lowest possible value** or **exactly at a breakpoint**.
> Investing in the middle ground between breakpoints results in wasted points.

---

### ❤️ Vigor

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **40** | HP gain efficiency drops sharply after this |
| **58** | The gain from 58 → 60 is minimal |

> Controls HP. Also affects Fire resistance and Immunity (Poison/Rot resistance).

> [!TIP]
> **Always invest to 40 minimum.** Going above 58 is not recommended.

---

### 💙 Mind

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **40** | Robustness (Sleep/Madness resistance) efficiency drops |
| **50** | FP diminishing returns begin |

> Controls FP (mana). Also affects Focus (Sleep/Madness resistance).

> [!TIP]
> If you use FP frequently, invest to **50**. Considering flask efficiency, **38** is also a solid stopping point.

---

### 💛 Endurance

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **25 / 60** | Equip Load growth stops |
| **40** | Robustness (Bleed/Frostbite resistance) efficiency drops |
| **50** | Stamina growth stops |

> Controls Stamina and Equip Load. Also affects Robustness (Bleed/Frostbite resistance).

> [!TIP]
> Spell-focused builds: **25**. Otherwise, minimum **30** recommended. For heavy Stamina users: aim for **50**.

---

### 🔨 Strength

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **20** | Strong early growth |
| **53** | Efficiency breakpoint |
| **80** | Soft cap |
| **35** *(two-handing)* | 1.5x bonus gives effective 52.5 Strength |

> Increases damage of Strength-scaling weapons. Also affects Physical defense.

> [!TIP]
> Meet weapon requirements, then rush to **20**. Aim for **35 (two-handing) or 53 (one-handing)** as a goal.
> **80 is not recommended** unless one-handing and committing fully.

---

### ⚡ Dexterity

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **20** | Strong early growth |
| **53** | Efficiency breakpoint |
| **80** | Sharp decline after this |

> Increases damage of Dexterity-scaling weapons. Reduces casting time, fall damage, and improves horse control.

> [!TIP]
> Invest to **20** first, build survivability, then aim for **53**. **80 is not recommended**.

---

### 🔮 Intelligence

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **20** | Magic resistance efficiency drops |
| **20 / 50 / 80** | Weapon attack breakpoints |
| **30 / 45** | Hybrid catalyst spell scaling breakpoints |
| **60 / 80** | Pure Intelligence catalyst breakpoints |

> Increases power of Intelligence-scaling sorceries. Also improves Magic resistance.

> [!TIP]
> Invest to **20** first, then commit fully to **50 or 80**.

---

### ✨ Faith

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **20 / 50 / 80** | Weapon attack breakpoints |
| **30 / 45** | Hybrid seal spell scaling breakpoints |
| **60 / 80** | Pure Faith seal breakpoints |

> Increases power of Faith-scaling incantations.

> [!TIP]
> Invest to **20** first, then commit fully to **50 or 80**.

---

### 🩸 Arcane

| Breakpoint | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **15 / 39** | Vitality (Death resistance) efficiency drops |
| **20** | Holy resistance efficiency drops |
| **44~46** | Bleed buildup plateaus |
| **30 / 45** | Hybrid catalyst spell scaling breakpoints |

> Controls Discovery. Also affects Holy resistance, Vitality (Death resistance), and certain sorceries/incantations.

> [!TIP]
> Invest to **20** first, then aim for **45**.
