## 💨 Ashes of War (AoW) — 3 Ash Rule

> [!IMPORTANT]
> All ashes of war are **legal**. However, you are limited to **3 uses of ANY ash of war per round** — across all ashes combined.

- Using any ash of war more than 3 times in a round counts as a **round loss**
- Being **interrupted** during the ash of war startup counts as a use
- **Followups** of an ash of war do not count as additional uses *(e.g. repeated presses of Stormblade without being interrupted, or the follow-up attack of Flaming Strike)*

| Example | Count |
|---------|:-----:|
| Flaming Strike L2 used once | 1 |
| Flaming Strike R2 followup | 0 *(followup)* |
| Stormblade: 3 presses, uninterrupted | 1 *(one use)* |
| Ash interrupted during startup | 1 |
| Any ash used a 4th time in a round | ❌ Round Loss |

**Counting responsibility:**
- **Tournaments** — Both players and TOs are expected to count. TOs have the final say
- **Ladder** — Players are encouraged to record. If neither player has a recording, disputes will be ignored

## 📋 General Rulings

> [!NOTE]
> During a Tourney, TOs withhold the right to reset a match if a new glitch that should've been blacklisted appears. Communicate with your TO **before the match** to avoid this.

| Rule |
|------|
| Breaking a rule or message heals will result in a round loss |
| For banned content, equipping them will be treated as intended use. *(Equipping a banned AoW but not using it is an exception)* |
| Unless permitted by the opponent, accidental use will be treated as intended use |
| Requests to show your build after the entire match has ended cannot be denied |
| The buff from greases must be gone before you swap to a setup that bans them |
| Pre-loading a Crossbow, Bow, or Ash of War is considered a Pre-Buff |
| Switching builds is prohibited once you enter the same world as your opponent |

> [!TIP]
> **Recording matches is highly recommended.**
