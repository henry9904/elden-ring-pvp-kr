## 📌 Basic Rules

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Rune Level** | 125 |
| **Respec** | Respecing or swapping character during a match is not allowed |
| **Terms of Service** | Adhere to the Elden Ring Terms of Service (e.g. no macros, no third party tools) |
| **Bugs / Glitches** | Using any blacklisted glitches or exploits is prohibited |
| **Connection Quality** | Hiding ping and connection quality is prohibited |

## 🐛 Blacklisted Glitches / Techs

---

## 📋 General Rulings

> [!NOTE]
> During a Tourney, TOs (Tourney Organizers) withhold the right to reset a match if a new glitch that should've been blacklisted appears. Communicate with your TO **before the match** to avoid this.

| Rule |
|------|
| Breaking a rule or message heals will result in a round loss |
| For banned content, equipping them will be treated as intended use. *(Equipping a banned AoW but not using it is an exception)* |
| Unless permitted by the opponent, accidental use will be treated as intended use |
| Requests to show your build after the entire match has ended cannot be denied |
| The buff from greases must be gone before you swap to a setup that bans them |
| Pre-loading a Crossbow, Bow, or Ash of War is considered a Pre-Buff |
| Switching builds is prohibited once you enter the same world as your opponent |

> [!TIP]
> **Recording matches is highly recommended.**
